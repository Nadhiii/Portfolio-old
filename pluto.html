Pluto

(Previously known as Finance Manager)

Because Your Wallet Deserves Better

Ever tried finding a finance app that just… works? Same here. That's why this one exists.

Why I Built This

I just wanted to track my income, expenses, debts, and maybe set a couple of goals — without giving some random app access to my SMS, email, and internal organs. 😅 Most apps either:

  • Demand a subscription before you can even test them
  • Want to read all your messages like a nosy roommate
  • Still don't do what you need after paying

So I said: "Fine. I'll build it myself." This is that app.

What It Does

  • 💰 Track Income, Expenses, Debts, Investments, Set Budgets & Goals
  • 🎯 Set Goals for savings, debt payoffs, or just surviving the month
  • 🔁 Sync everything to Google Sheets — perfect for spreadsheet nerds (like me)
  • ☁️ Works across devices (as long as you sign in with the same Google account)
  • 🌓 Light and dark modes, because some of us track finances at 2 AM

No Nonsense

  • No SMS reading.
  • No bank scraping.
  • No subscriptions.
  • No surprise ads trying to sell you crypto.

Just you, your money, and your data.

First App

This is my first ever app. I've put a lot into making sure everything works the way it should — but hey, I'm human. If you spot bugs, have feature ideas, or just want to say hi — feel free to ping me!

I Didn't Build This Alone

Big thanks to ChatGPT and Claude — my AI coding buddies — for helping me debug, brainstorm, and occasionally explain stuff like I'm five. Couldn't have done it without them.
