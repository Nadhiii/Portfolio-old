Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. We use three types of cookies:

  • Necessary cookies: These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.
  • Analytics cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. They help us improve our website and services.
  • Marketing cookies: These cookies are used to track visitors across websites. They are set to display targeted advertisements and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

You can control your cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept all cookies, or only necessary cookies. You can also change your preferences at any time by clicking the cookie preferences button at the bottom right of the page.
